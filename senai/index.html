Kiki's Delivery Service

The story centers around Kiki, a young witch who, upon turning 13, must leave her home to find her place in the world and make a living.

Coraline

The film tells the story of its eponymous character discovering an idealized alternate universe behind a secret door in her new home, unaware that it contains something dark and sinister.

Alice In Wonderland

Alice is a young girl who uses her surreal imagination to escape her mundane life. Alice's curiosity and yearn for something new leads her to a mysterious realm known as Wonderland. As she traverses through the wacky world, Alice finds herself appreciating the normality that she once rejected.
